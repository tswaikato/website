Training Ship Waikato, also known as Waikato Navy Cadet's, is a Unit of the New Zealand Cadet Forces. Training children aged 13 to 18 years of age we deliver our content through our weekly parade nights and weekend activities.

What We Do

Our training focus's around leadership and will challenge you to lead your peers to achieve tasks. To compliment this leadership focus we train do activities such as Camping, Tramping, Sailing & Powerboats, and Range shooting During your time as a cadet you will likely also visit Defence Force bases and partake in ceremonies where you are seen to represent the Defence Force.

Courage

Confronting challenges, both physical and moral, to overcome any adversity.

Comradeship

Looking out for each other, having respect for all, and championing the benefits of friendship, teamwork and diversity.

Commitment

Being reliable and loyally serving and supporting the NZCF, local communities and New Zealand.

Integrity

Having self discipline and always being honest, trustworthy and responsible.
