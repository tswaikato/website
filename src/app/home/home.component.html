Training Ship Waikato, also known as Waikato Navy Cadet’s is one of the three non-profit branches of the New Zealand Cadet Forces and is aimed at persons aged 13 to 18 years of age. The main focus for our cadet’s is military-based leadership and personal development through a range of both national and regional courses providing experience and qualifications, along with our weekly parade nights and weekend activities.

Where To Find Us?

You can find us at 6 Cambridge Road Hamilton where we hold our weekly parade nights.
