Courage

Confronting challenges, both physical and moral, to overcome any adversity.

Commitment

Being reliable and loyally serving and supporting the NZCF, local communities and New Zealand.

Comradeship

Looking out for each other, having respect for all, and championing the benefits of friendship, teamwork and diversity

Integrity

Having self discipline and always being honest, trustworthy and responsible.

Training Ship Waikato, also known as Waikato Navy Cadet’s is one of the three non-profit branches of the New Zealand Cadet Forces and is aimed at persons aged 13 to 18 years of age. The main focus for our cadet’s is military-base leadership and personal development through a range of both national and regional courses providing experience and qualifications, along with our weekly parade nights and weekend activities.
