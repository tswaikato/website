LEADERSHIP

Leadership is a core function of our organisation; Cadets begin at the bottom and over time move their way up the ranks. This gives real world experience to managing people, a skill that is greatly sought after by employers in the future and something that the school system doesn’t actively offer. After 2 – 3 years involved cadets will spend time confirming the skills they have learned through a week long course typically held at a defence establishment, this course is full paid by the defence force.

SAILING

A large element of a Navy Cadets career will be learning to sail. We use sailing to promote and encourage team work, this is done via weekend exercises and also competing in the National Regatta where our unit is put to the test in a number of challenges all based around our training syllabus to see which unit is the best in the country.

FIREARMS TRAINING

Cadets are trained in the safe handling and operation of firearms, before operating cadets are training the firearms safety laws and also rules around owning a firearm. During their time in Cadets they will likely partake in many range days and have the chance to competitively shoot. NZCF are only authorised to shoot .22 rifles and only at circular targets on closed / Defence Ranges. In some instances, Cadet may also be invited to participate in shooting exercises with NZDF directly but only if they can demonstrate sound understanding of the rules.

TEAMWORK

We pride ourselves in every we do to be enabling the Cadet to grow as a member and also a leader of a team. During their time with us they will be challenged but growth is typically enabled by the comradeship of those around them and they friend’s they also make from other units.

NZCF Officers

NZCF officers are all volunteers that truly believe in the value this organisation brings to our communities and next generation of New Zealanders. If you think you would like to get involved as a volunteer / officer please email us ts.waikato@cadetforces.org.nz
All NZCF Officers are subject to our NZCF 7 Criminal Records check process and this is refreshed every 3 years.
